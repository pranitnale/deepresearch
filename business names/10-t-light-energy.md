## Collision + language screens

### Tejomani — VERDICT: CLEAR — "jewel of light"
- **Construction:** tejas (radiance) + maṇi (jewel/gem) — a natural Sanskrit compound, used as a rare spiritual/personal name; not a dictionary headword but reads as genuine.
- **Collisions:** none found. Nearest phonetic echo: Teji Mandi (Indian retail-investing app, Motilal Oswal) — different words, different rhythm, India-only, finance.
- **Language:** clean across the 7-language screen. Say it: DE "te-yo-MA-ni", EN "te-jo-MAH-ni" (the J-split from 09 applies). 4 syllables — one above the 1–3 guideline, but every syllable is open CV and the spelling is recoverable in both languages.
- **.com + .ai + .de all available.**

### Tapanwerk — VERDICT: CLEAR — "sun-works"
- **Construction:** tapana (the sun, the warming one) + *Werk*.
- **Collisions:** no exact hit; nothing close found.
- **Language & phonetics — the standout:** *no letter shifts at all* between German and English — t, a, p, a, n are read identically in both, making this phonetically the cleanest -werk compound of all waves (Urjawerk has the ū, Tejawerk the J-split, Mitrawerk the mitre noun). Say it: ta-PAN-verk everywhere. Plosive T start (recall bonus per 01). Mild echo of Spanish *tapa* (snack) — indirect and food-neutral.
- **.com + .ai + .de all available.**

### Tejomana — VERDICT: CLEAR (pure coinage)
- **Construction — honest note:** NOT dictionary Sanskrit. Coined here from tejas (light) + manas (mind) → "light-mind" — arguably the most on-mission constructed meaning for an AI company with an energy goal. Present it as a coinage, not a real word.
- **Collisions:** none found (unscreened beyond the Tejomani search overlap — do a dedicated search before committing).
- **.com + .ai + .de all available.**

### Tapana — MINOR RISK (.ai-first)
- Collisions: a dormant-looking TAPANA LTD (UK companies register); Tapana (Telugu film). No tech/energy user found.
- Language: Finnish *tapana* is an everyday grammatical form ("as a habit") — neutral, and Finnish is outside the 7-language screen. .com taken.

### Taijasa — MINOR (.ai-first, exotic)
