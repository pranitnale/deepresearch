# Business Names — Company Naming Research

Research and naming work (2026-07-13) for a planned **Germany-based B2B parent company** that builds custom AI applications for businesses across sectors, with the ultimate mission of **driving AI adoption in the European energy sector**. The founder is an Indian citizen and renewable-energy engineer living in Germany.

## Naming brief

- Name should have an **Indian/Sanskrit root**, but be easy for Europeans — especially German speakers — to pronounce, spell after hearing once, and remember.
- Pure Sanskrit not required: suffixes (-x, -ai, -on, …) and Indian+German or Indian+English hybrids are allowed.
- Parent-company name: must stay sector-agnostic (AI products across sectors, energy as the ultimate goal).
- Target customers: European businesses. Exact-match `.com` or `.ai` domain desired.

## Status / result

- Wave 1–2 recommendation: **Urjawerk** (ūrjā "energy" + German *Werk*) — verification-clear, .com/.ai/.de all available (re-verified 2026-07-13).
- **Wave 3 (founder wanted more-unique options)** added three more full-stack-available, collision-clear candidates: **Sumarga** ("the good path" — most sector-agnostic), **Mitrawerk** (ally + Werk), **Kiranwerk** (ray of light + Werk); details and ranking in 08.
- **Wave 3b (founder asked for tej-based names):** **Sutejas** (real Sanskrit, "very radiant" — no collisions, .com/.ai/.de all free) and **Tejawerk** (radiance + Werk, all free); details in 09.
- **Wave 3c (T-starting light/energy names):** **Tapanwerk** (tapana "the sun" + Werk — zero letter-shift between DE/EN, best pure phonetics of the project) and **Tejomani** (tejas + maṇi, "jewel of light") — both .com/.ai/.de free; details in 10.
- **Wave 3d (tivra coinages):** **Tivrium** (tīvra "intense" + -ium — collision-clear, .com/.ai/.de all free), Tivran and Tivrawerk also full-stack; details in 11.
- `.ai`-first alternatives: Medhira, Ritama, Suryana, Urjon, Ojasva (07); Sunaya, Sunetra, Tarini (08); Tejomaya (09); Tapana, Taijasa (10).
- Dropped: Krantix, Kiranix (05); Sumati, Agrima, Utsaha, Pramita, Sudaksha (08); Tejora, Tejasva, Tejwerk-vs-Teewerk caveat (09); Taraka (10); Tivron, Tivrix, Tivrus, Tivrea (11).
