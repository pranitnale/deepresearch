# Domain Availability Check

Checked 2026-07-13.

**Method note:** The session's GoDaddy connector hit its daily search quota (shared IP, resets midnight UTC), and the sandbox network policy blocked direct RDAP queries. Status below was therefore determined via **NS-record lookups against the authoritative DNS registries** (dnspython, system resolver; NXDOMAIN-hijacking ruled out with a control query). Interpretation: `REGISTERED` (NS delegation exists) is definitive; `AVAILABLE` strictly means "not delegated in the TLD zone" — near-certain available for .com/.ai and effectively certain for .de (DENIC requires working NS at registration). **Re-verify at a registrar immediately before purchase.** A GoDaddy confirmation was scheduled for 00:15 UTC 2026-07-14 in the original session.

## Key takeaways

1. Every *pure* Sanskrit dictionary word checked is taken on both .com and .ai — coinages or hybrids are required for exact-match domains.
2. **Only Urjawerk and Setuwerk are fully available across .com/.ai/.de.**
3. An `.ai`-first strategy (normal for AI companies, cf. mistral.ai) opens: urjon.ai, medhira.ai, ritama.ai, suryana.ai, ojasva.ai.
4. Aftermarket (premium purchase) options for pure roots: dhara.ai, disha.com+.ai, mitra.ai, suryana.com.
