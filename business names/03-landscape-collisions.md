# Naming Landscape: Companies, Trends, Collisions

## Sanskrit-named companies (precedents and collision risks)

### Samsara Inc. — IoT / physical-operations SaaS (fleet telematics), NYSE: IOT

Biggest collision risk. Sanskrit for the interconnected cycle of life/death; founded 2015, IPO 2021, a dominant global B2B SaaS brand. The 'samsara' root is effectively owned in enterprise software. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsara_(company)

### Asana — Work/project-management SaaS

Major collision. Sanskrit for 'posture/seat' (yoga term); one of the best-known B2B productivity brands, so 'asana' as a software name reads as taken. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asana and https://www.dictionary.com/browse/asana

### Indra Sistemas — IT / defense / transport tech (Spain, Europe-facing)

Critical collision for a Europe-facing B2B company: a large, established European tech/defense group named after the Vedic god Indra (associated with power/leadership). The exact Sanskrit gloss is unverified in sources, but the god-name origin is confirmed. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indra_Sistemas

### Tejas Networks — Telecom/optical networking equipment (India, Tata group)

Precedent + collision. 'Tejas' = radiance/fire/energy in Sanskrit; a listed telecom-infra company. https://matrixbcg.com/blogs/brief-history/tejasnetworks and https://www.wisdomlib.org/definition/tejas

### Manas AI — AI-native biopharmaceutical

Corrects the brief: co-founded by Siddhartha Mukherjee, Reid Hoffman and Ujjwal Singh — no verified Vinod Khosla role. 'Manas' = mind in Sanskrit; useful precedent (mind/cognition metaphor for an AI company). https://manasai.co/company and https://www.wisdomlib.org/definition/manas

### Vayu Robotics — AI robotics / autonomous delivery (Palo Alto)

Root now tied to a funded AI-robotics brand, acquired by Serve Robotics in Aug 2025. 'Vayu' = wind / Hindu wind god. Collision for AI/robotics naming. https://www.vayurobotics.com/ and https://serverobotics.gcs-web.com/news-releases/news-release-details/serve-robotics-acquires-vayu-robotics-pioneer-ai-foundation

### Arka Energy (and multiple 'Arka' solar firms) — Solar / renewable energy

Directly in your target sector. 'Arka' = sun/radiance in Sanskrit; used by Arka Energy plus several Indian solar firms (Arka Solutions, Arka Solar). Crowded in energy. https://www.arkaenergy.com/about and https://www.wisdomlib.org/names/arka

### Vyoma (Vyoma GmbH) — Space situational awareness / satellite operations (Munich, Germany)

Strong precedent: a German deep-tech startup with a Sanskrit name that Europeans pronounce fine. 'Vyoma' = sky/space/ether; thematically matched to its business. https://www.vyoma.space/ and https://www.munich-startup.de/en/startups/vyoma-gmbh/

### VEDA GmbH — HR & payroll software (Alsdorf, Germany, founded 1977)

Collision in German B2B software specifically. 'Veda' = knowledge (Sanskrit); an established ~1,200-customer German software vendor, majority-acquired by Investcorp. https://www.veda.net/ and https://www.software-made-in-germany.org/unternehmen/veda-gmbh/

### Sama (formerly Samasource) — Data annotation / labeling for AI

Adjacent to your space (AI data). Founded 2008 by Leila Janah; rebranded to 'Sama' in 2021. The Sanskrit reading ('sama' = equal/even) is unverified — sources do not document the name's etymology. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sama_(company)

### Sarvam AI — Sovereign LLM / foundation models (Bengaluru)

Precedent for AI + Sanskrit. 'Sarvam' = all/whole; selected by India's MeitY to build a sovereign LLM. https://restofworld.org/2026/india-frugal-ai-sarvam-krutrim-sovereign/ and https://founderpin.com/startup_story/sarvam-ai/

### Krutrim — Generative-AI / LLM (Ola, India)

Precedent: 'Krutrim' literally means 'artificial' in Sanskrit — India's first AI unicorn. Shows the artificial/AI-meaning naming angle is already claimed. https://techcrunch.com/2024/01/26/ola-founder-ai-startup-krutrim-unicorn-in-50m-funding/

### Druva — Data-protection / backup SaaS (US, Indian founders)

Precedent for a Europe/US-facing B2B SaaS with a Sanskrit name explained on-brand. From 'Dhruva' = pole star, chosen to signal steadfast reliability. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Druva

### Oorja / Urja (multiple firms) — Renewable energy, battery analytics/ML

Directly your sector and crowded: Oorja Development Solutions (solar for farms), oorja.energy (physics+ML battery analytics), Urja Global (solar EPC). 'Urja/Oorja' = energy. https://oorja.energy/ and https://tracxn.com/d/companies/oorja/__VweKa5DQdeN1ULg3PF2ssVLxdUI7UsgK9GiTY-yfP3M

### Medha Servo Drives — Rail traction/vehicle electronics (Hyderabad, India)

'Medha' = intellect/wisdom (fitting for AI) but already an established industrial brand; note a separate global rail-electronics brand also trades as 'Medha'. https://medha.com/ and https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/medha-servo-drives

### Yantra / Yantr.ai / TestYantra / Yantra AI — IT consulting, ML&AI, field-service automation, QA

Heavily oversaturated in the AI/automation consulting space you are entering. 'Yantra' = machine/instrument; used by a consulting+ML firm, a Tech Mahindra field-service product (Yantr.ai), TestYantra, and industrial-AI Yantra AI. https://www.techmahindra.com/services/business-process-services/bpaas/yantrai/ and https://www.yantraai.in/

### Setu — Fintech API infrastructure (India)

'Setu' = bridge (Sanskrit) — an on-brand naming precedent for connective/infrastructure products; acquired by Pine Labs in 2025, so the root is claimed in fintech infra. https://setu.co/about/ and https://www.fintechfutures.com/fintech-start-ups/india-s-pine-labs-acquires-api-fintech-start-up-setu

### Tarka (tarka.ai) / Tarka Labs / Tark AI — AI GTM agents; custom generative-AI app development

Direct overlap with 'custom AI applications for business.' tarka.ai deploys custom GTM AI agents (Raleigh); Tarka Labs (Chennai) explicitly builds custom generative-AI apps. 'Tarka' = logic/reasoning is a plausible but unverified gloss. Avoid — closest to your exact positioning. https://tarka.ai/ and https://tarkalabs.com/genai/

### Allcargo Gati — Express logistics / supply chain (India)

'Gati' = motion/speed/progression; the name was picked from a shortlist of Sanskrit words to signal speed-with-direction. Established logistics brand. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Allcargo_Gati_Ltd and https://www.wisdomlib.org/definition/gati

### Electromotion e-Vidyut — EV retrofit / electric mobility (India)

'Vidyut' = lightning, and electricity in modern Hindi — an on-the-nose energy/electrification name already in use in EV. https://evidyut.in/ and https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%BF%E0%A4%A6%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AF%E0%A5%81%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%8D

### Meru Networks — Enterprise Wi-Fi / WLAN (Sunnyvale; acquired by Fortinet 2015)

'Meru' = the mythic central mountain / 'high' in Sanskrit; a known enterprise-networking brand now inside Fortinet, so the root carries B2B-tech baggage. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Meru_Networks and https://sanskritdictionary.org/meru

### Ojas (IIIT-Hyderabad incubator) — MedTech incubator

Comparatively OPEN root: 'ojas' = vitality/vigor; no dominant energy or AI company found using it, only a health-tech incubator. Viable candidate. https://www.iiit.ac.in/ojas/ and https://www.yogapedia.com/definition/5328/ojas

### Netra / Netradyne / DRDO Netra — Computer vision, fleet-safety AI, defense drone

Partly used: 'netra' = eye. Netradyne (vision-based fleet safety) and India's DRDO Netra drone occupy the vision/surveillance niche, but no single dominant 'Netra' B2B brand — usable outside vision/defense. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DRDO_Netra

### Ritam Technologies — Niche vertical software (US)

Root largely OPEN for AI/energy: 'ritam' (ṛtam) = cosmic order/truth. Only user found is a small US vertical-software firm, so no prominent collision. https://www.ritamglobal.com/ and https://www.wisdomlib.org/names/ritam

### Dhara — (no significant tech/energy company found)

Effectively OPEN root: 'dhara' = flow/stream/earth (connotes continuity). No notable tech, AI or energy company identified using it as a brand. https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E0%A4%A7%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%B0%E0%A4%BE and https://www.wisdomlib.org/definition/dhara

### Airbus Vahana — eVTOL / urban air mobility (Airbus, Europe; project ended 2019)

Precedent, not a collision: a major European aerospace group openly used a Sanskrit name ('vahana' = vehicle), evidence Sanskrit names are accepted in European B2B/industrial branding. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Airbus_A%C2%B3_Vahana

## Naming trends: AI and energy sector

- The '.ai' domain and 'AI' string are ubiquitous and oversaturated in AI naming, placed at the start, middle or end of names (e.g. Yellow.ai, Fireflies.ai, Clarifai) — it instantly signals the category but no longer differentiates. https://customerthink.com/in-the-bubble-naming-an-ai-startup-in-2025/
- Vowel-dropping (Flickr/Tumblr lineage) is still used in tech/AI naming but is now widely seen as clichéd. https://customerthink.com/in-the-bubble-naming-an-ai-startup-in-2025/
- Coined names from Greek/Latin roots remain a dominant strategy (the Kodak/Xerox/Verizon tradition), including European AI leaders like Mistral. https://riverandwolf.com/what-to-call-it-trends-in-ai-naming/ and https://www.aibizname.com/blog/unique-ai-startup-name-ideas-for-startups
- Human-first-name and short, warm, evocative single words are a live 2025 trend (Jasper, Alexa, Arthur; Lovable, Mistral) — an emotional-brand counterweight to technical names. https://www.aibizname.com/blog/unique-ai-startup-name-ideas-for-startups and https://customerthink.com/in-the-bubble-naming-an-ai-startup-in-2025/
- A visible swing back toward plain, real-word 'normal' names after years of invented/misspelled brands. https://marker.medium.com/startups-are-starting-to-choose-normal-names-again-4816e4a2834d
- European AI companies skew abstract/metaphorical or lab-suffixed rather than descriptive: Mistral, Lovable, Black Forest Labs, Prem Labs, ElevenLabs, n8n — the '…Labs' suffix in particular is common and getting crowded. https://www.eu-startups.com/2025/05/the-ai-uprising-20-european-startups-rewriting-the-rules-in-2025/ and https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/02/beyond-lovable-and-mistral-21-european-startups-to-watch/
- Energy/cleantech naming is dominated by nature imagery (sun, wind, light) and two-word compound names — SunPower, BrightSource, Sunrun, Enphase, and in Europe Northvolt — making sun/solar/volt/watt morphemes heavily oversaturated. https://smartbranding.com/renewable-energy-companies-and-their-domain-name-choices/ and https://www.brandbucket.com/name-ideas/renewable-energy-business-names
- Sanskrit roots are an established, accepted naming pool in both global tech and European industry (Samsara, Asana, Druva, Indra, Airbus Vahana, Munich's Vyoma), so a Sanskrit root is credible in Europe — but the most obvious/thematic roots in energy and AI are already claimed. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samsara_(company) and https://www.munich-startup.de/en/startups/vyoma-gmbh/
