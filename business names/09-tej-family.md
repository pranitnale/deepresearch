## The structural trade-off of any tej- name

The letter **J always splits the two target audiences** (04 rule 2): Germans read *tej* as "tey", English speakers as "tedge/tayj". There is no spelling that avoids this while keeping the root visible. The workable names are those where **both audiences can still recover the same spelling after one hearing** — intervocalic j is natural for Germans to write (Maja, Kajak, Boje), so `-eja-` names spell reliably; a bare `tej-` before a consonant is riskier.

Trivia to be aware of: *tej* is also the Ethiopian honey wine (t'ej) — neutral-to-positive, known mainly in foodie circles. And every tej- name shares its root with Tejas Networks (Indian telecom infra) — different sector, but include it in the DPMA/EUIPO similarity check.

## Collision + language screens

### Sutejas — VERDICT: CLEAR — top tej pick
- **What it is:** a genuine Sanskrit dictionary compound, *su-tejas* = "very bright, very radiant, potent" (wisdomlib, sanskritdictionary.com); also a rare personal name (fewer than 5 occurrences/year in US SSA data).
- **Collisions:** none found — search returns only dictionaries and name-meaning sites. No company anywhere.
- **Language:** clean in the 7-language screen; no German meaning; "su-" prefix echoes the other su- candidates (Sumarga, Sunaya).
- **Say it:** DE "su-TE-yas", EN "soo-TAY-jas". Spelling recovery good in both (intervocalic j).
- **Why it's strong:** it is *real* Sanskrit (not a coinage) yet behaves like a fanciful mark in Europe; keeps the full *tejas* root visible; 3 syllables; .com + .ai + .de all free.

### Tejawerk — VERDICT: CLEAR
- **Collisions:** no exact hit. Near-names: TEQWERK GmbH (Bavarian cloud-transformation consultancy — but "Teq" reads /tek/, clearly distinct from /te-ya/), Tegowerk, Textilwerk (unrelated).
- **Language:** clean; follows the proven Urjawerk/Kraftwerk pattern — *tejas* + *Werk* = "radiance-works".
- **Say it:** DE "te-YA-verk", EN "te-JAH-werk". The linking -a- makes German spelling recovery reliable (Maja pattern).

### Tejwerk — VERDICT: MINOR RISK (deprioritized vs Tejawerk)
- **Collisions:** no exact hit, but two German near-homophones: **TEQWERK GmbH** (German IT/cloud consulting — same country, adjacent sector) and *Teewerk* (German "tea works" — a real shop name pattern). German hearing "TEY-verk" may write **Teewerk** — a one-hearing spelling failure (04 rule 11).
- Tejawerk is the same idea without the mishearing problem; prefer it.

### Tejomaya — VERDICT: MINOR RISK (.ai-first only)
- *tejomaya* = "made of light / consisting of radiance" — arguably the most beautiful meaning in the entire research.
- **Collisions:** Tejomaya Resorts (Jim Corbett, India — jungle retreat + homestay). Non-tech, India-only; the .com is registered but with broken DNS delegation (worth watching).
- **Caveats:** 4 syllables (above the 1–3 guideline), spiritual overtone; te-jo-MA-ya.

### Tejora — VERDICT: AVOID
- **Tejora Private Limited / Tejora Technologies** (Mumbai, since 2003, acquired by PrimeSoft 2021) — custom software development for banks/insurers. Identical name, direct sector collision.

### Tejasva / Tejasvi — VERDICT: AVOID
- Multiple small Indian firms (Tejasvi Industries, Tejasvi/Tejasva Enterprises, Tejaswi Group conglomerate) plus Tejasvi Surya (prominent Indian MP) — crowded, name-like, and the -sva/-svi ending adds the V letter-shift for Germans.

### Tejon — VERDICT: AVOID
- Spanish *tejón* = badger; Tejon Ranch (major US company); domains taken anyway.

### Unscreened but domain-open (.ai/.de): Tejasa, Tejova, Tejaron, Tejam
- Not collision-searched. Tejova carries a double letter-shift for Germans (j→y AND v→f: "te-YO-fa") — weakest of the four on 04 rules.

## Tej-family ranking

1. **Sutejas** — real Sanskrit ("very radiant"), zero collisions, .com/.ai/.de all free. The strongest way to own *tej*.
2. **Tejawerk** — the Indo-German compound route ("radiance-works"), all three domains free, no collisions.
3. **Tejwerk** — available, but Teewerk-mishearing + TEQWERK GmbH proximity make Tejawerk the better spelling of the same idea.
4. Tejomaya (.ai-first, resort collision, long but beautiful).

Dropped: Tejora, Tejasva/Tejasvi, Tejon.
