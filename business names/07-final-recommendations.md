# Final Name Recommendations

Delivered 2026-07-13. Basis: research in files 01–04, verification in 05, domains in 06.

## Top recommendation: Urjawerk

**ūrjā (ऊर्जा, "energy") + German *Werk* (works/plant)** — say it: OOR-ya-verk, identical reading in German and English.

- It literally *is* the mission: an energy-works. Echoes *Kraftwerk*, instantly credible to German Mittelstand and utilities, and the Indo-German compound mirrors the founder's own story (Indian roots, German market).
- Scores on nearly every research principle: 3 syllables, one unambiguous spelling, fanciful/coined trademark strength, unlimited room for sub-brands (Urjawerk Grid, Urjawerk Labs, …).
- **Domains: urjawerk.com, urjawerk.ai, urjawerk.de — all available.**
- Risks: cleanest of all nine verified candidates. Swiss watchmaker URWERK is a phonetic neighbor (different sector); "Urja" is a common root among Indian energy brands.

**Buy-it-instead:** pure roots **dhara.ai** (flow), **disha.com/.ai** (direction/guidance), **mitra.ai** (ally + sun — strong B2B double meaning) are parked on aftermarket exchanges and purchasable at a premium.

## Why a Sanskrit root is strategically right (not just personal)

- A Sanskrit word reads as a *coined/fanciful* mark in Europe → strongest EUIPO trademark tier.
- Proven precedent in European B2B: Samsara, Asana, Druva, Airbus *Vahana*, Munich's *Vyoma*.
- Keeps the parent company sector-agnostic (Alphabet logic) — no "AI"/"energy" hard-coded into the legal name; signal AI via the `.ai` domain and sub-brands instead.
